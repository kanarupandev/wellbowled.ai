## Key Decisions (Locked)

### Strategic
- **Position:** Specialist bowling analysis brand, not AI app account
- **Channels:** Instagram Reels (primary) + YouTube Shorts (second primary)
- **Audience first 90 days:** Serious bowlers 14-30 + cricket analysis fans
- **Pillars:** Technique Proof, Pro Deconstruction, App Proof, Progress/Community
- **Women's cricket:** Dedicated lane from day 1
- **Language:** English voice + Hindi/Tamil subtitle line

### Visual Identity
- **Lead visual:** Skeleton overlay with phase-based color coding (green/amber/red)
- **Top 3 techniques:** Kinogram, Goniogram (angle arcs), Hip-Shoulder Separation (X-Factor)
- **Brand color:** Peacock Blue #006D77
- **Watermark:** Bottom-corner peacock blue pill

### Content Formula
- **Hook:** 0-1s — bold claim or striking visual
- **Proof:** 1-8s — skeleton overlay + targeted technique (angles, trails, X-factor)
- **Verdict:** 8-12s — phase grades or improvement callout
- **CTA:** 12-15s — follow / scan QR / submit your clip

### Measurement (rank order)
1. Share rate
2. Save rate
3. Completion rate
4. Qualified comments
5. Follows per 1K views
