# Content Production Plan

## Goal

Produce short-form bowling analysis clips for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts that showcase wellBowled.ai's analysis capabilities and cricket bowling technique insights.

---

## Tool Stack

### Primary Editor
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free) — editing, color grading, motion graphics via Fusion
- Download: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve

### Quick Vertical Clips
- **CapCut Desktop** (free) — auto-captions, templates, speed curves, vertical-first
- Download: https://www.capcut.com

### AI Enhancement
- **Runway ML** — AI slo-mo interpolation (30fps -> 120fps), background removal
- **Descript** — edit video by editing transcript, great for voice-over breakdowns
- **Opus Clip** — AI auto-picks best moments from long sessions

### Annotations / Graphics
- **Apple Motion** ($50) or **Canva** (free) — animated overlays, speed dials, phase labels
- DaVinci Fusion (built-in) — angle lines, body position markers drawn on video

### Screen Recording (app demos)
- **Screen Studio** ($89) — smooth zoom, device frames

## Clip Formula

### Structure (under 15 seconds for Reels/Shorts)

| Segment     | Duration | Content                                              |
|-------------|----------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Hook        | 0-2s     | Bold claim + freeze frame ("This is why X works")    |
| Breakdown   | 3-8s     | Slo-mo with drawn lines, phase labels, side-by-side  |
| Payoff      | 8-12s    | Full speed delivery, speed overlay, drill CTA         |
| CTA         | 12-15s   | "Follow for more" or app mention                     |

### Formats

| Platform        | Aspect | Duration  | Notes                          |
|-----------------|--------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| Instagram Reels | 9:16   | 7-15s     | Hook in first 1s, captions on  |
| TikTok          | 9:16   | 7-15s     | Trending audio helps reach     |
| YouTube Shorts  | 9:16   | 15-60s    | Can go slightly longer         |
| YouTube         | 16:9   | 3-10 min  | Deep dive technique breakdowns |
| Twitter/X       | 16:9   | 30-90s    | Shorter, punchier              |

## Filming Best Practices

| Factor       | Recommendation                                          |
|--------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| Camera       | iPhone 15 rear, 4K/60fps or 1080p/240fps slo-mo       |
| Angle        | Side-on, perpendicular to pitch (arm side)             |
| Height       | Stump height (~71cm), use tripod                       |
| Distance     | 10-15m for full action, 3-5m for release detail        |
| Lighting     | Daylight outdoors, or well-lit nets                    |
| Background   | Uncluttered (helps AI background removal)              |
| Orientation  | Landscape for raw, crop to vertical in post            |
| Audio        | Separate voice-over in post (cleaner than field audio) |

---

## Workflow

1. **Film** — iPhone 4K/60fps side-on at stump height
2. **Transfer** — AirDrop to Mac, drop in `raw/`
3. **Script** — Write hook/breakdown/payoff in `scripts/`
4. **Edit** — DaVinci Resolve for polished clips, CapCut for quick ones
5. **Enhance** — Runway for slo-mo interpolation if needed
6. **Caption** — CapCut auto-captions or Descript
7. **Export** — To `exports/`, named by platform and date
8. **Post** — Schedule across platforms
