## Purpose

This is a **generic PDS** (not specific to the RAV4 purchase). It forms part of the insurance contract together with the Policy Schedule. It outlines the product terms, conditions, benefits, exclusions, and claims process for Toyota Comprehensive Motor Vehicle Insurance.

## Comprehensive Insurance Cover

### Core Cover
- **Agreed Value:** The amount shown on the policy schedule, covers the vehicle for the entire period of cover. Maximum payout in a total loss.
- **Accidental Loss or Damage:** Covers accidental loss or damage (including fire and theft). Toyota will arrange repair, pay repair costs, or declare total loss and pay agreed value.
- **Third Party Property Damage:** Legal liability for loss or damage to other people's property from an accident involving your vehicle or substitute vehicle. Maximum legal liability: **$20 million**.
- **Accessories & Non-Performance Modifications:** Covered within the agreed value.

### Driving Instruction Cover
- Learner drivers covered during supervised driving practice (no age/licence excess on learner)
- After gaining probationary licence, driver must be nominated on policy

### Glass Cover
- Windscreen, sunroof glass, window glass repair/replacement with genuine parts meeting Australian Design Rules

### New Vehicle Replacement Conditions (Toyota Vehicles)
- **Within first 3 years of original registration:** New replacement vehicle of same make/model/series if insurance was purchased within 12 months of original registration and continuously insured
- **If insurance NOT purchased within 12 months:** Replacement only within first 2 years of original registration
- Replacement vehicle includes all on-road costs
- Financier consent required if vehicle is under finance

### Toyota Finance Extended Total Loss Cover
- For Toyota Access financed vehicles with original loan term 3+ years, within 12 months of original registration
- Extends "Replacement with new vehicle" benefit to the greater of: first 3 years of registration OR original Toyota Access contract term (up to 4 years max)

### Toyota Finance Payout Gap Benefit
- If finance payout exceeds agreed value after total loss, Toyota Insurance pays up to **$10,000** toward the outstanding finance balance
- Excludes arrears, deferred payments, and penalty costs

## Exclusions

### General Exclusions
- Intentional/reckless damage by you or someone acting with your knowledge
- Wear and tear, rust, corrosion, depreciation
- Mechanical, electrical, electronic breakdown/failure
- Tyre damage from braking, road punctures, cuts, bursts (unless concurrent with an accident)
- Consequential loss (loss of income, business interruption, depreciation from accident history)
- Hazardous goods transport
- Vehicle used while defective/unroadworthy (if you knew or should have known)
- Lawful seizure/government action
- War, terrorism, nuclear events
- Pollution/contamination
- Cyber events

### Vehicle Exclusions
- Unregistered vehicle (unless just purchased or registration lapsed <14 days)
- Racing, speed/time trials, rallies, track days
- Towing for reward
- Carrying passengers for reward (excl. approved rideshare)
- Used outside Australia

### Driver Exclusions
- Unlicensed driver / licence doesn't cover the vehicle
- Driver under influence of drugs or alcohol (>prescribed limit)
- Driver refuses breath/blood/drug test
- Driver convicted of stealing the vehicle

## Making a Claim

1. Contact Toyota Insurance: **1300 658 027**
2. Report to police if theft, malicious damage, or crime involved
3. Do not authorise repairs without Toyota Insurance approval
4. Do not admit liability
5. Provide all documents and information requested

### Excess Application
- Basic excess always applies
- Age excess applies if driver is under 25
- Licence excess applies based on licence type
- Multiple excesses can stack
- No excess for: windscreen/glass claims (if optional excess-free glass purchased), not-at-fault claims with identified third party

## Claims Settlement

### Damaged Vehicle
- Toyota Insurance chooses: repair, pay repair cost, or declare total loss
- **Lifetime Repair Guarantee** on authorised repairs
- Genuine parts used where available
- If repair cost approaches agreed value, may declare total loss

### Total Loss
- Pay agreed value less any excess and amounts owing
- If financed: pay financier first, remainder to insured

## Additional Terms & Conditions

### Rental Cars
- Must be Toyota/Lexus vehicle or similar if unavailable
- Standard insurance conditions apply to rental car

### Paying Premium
- Annual or monthly instalments
- If monthly payment fails, 14 days to pay before potential cancellation

### Renewing Policy
- Written invitation to renew before expiry
- May not renew if risk has changed

### Cancelling Policy
- **21-day cooling off** with full refund (if no claim made)
- After 21 days: refund of unexpired premium less processing charge
- Toyota Insurance can cancel with 14 days written notice

### Change of Vehicle
- Must notify Toyota Insurance
- New policy schedule issued
