## Observations & Notes

### Demo Vehicle
The RAV4 was a dealer demo registered ~26/03/2025 with 5 km. The buyer gets ~11 months of registration instead of 12 since rego was already active before purchase.

### Business vs Private Usage Discrepancy
- **Loan application** declares the vehicle is for "predominantly business purposes" (required for business finance)
- **Insurance policy** declares vehicle usage as "Private"
- These should be consistent - if challenged, one or both could be problematic

### Company Age
AAS Security Group Pty Ltd was registered on 02/01/2024, making it approximately 15 months old at the time of the loan application. This is a very young company.

### Guarantor Structure
Barath Loganathan is personally liable for the entire loan as both the sole director of the borrowing company and as personal guarantor. The guarantee is unconditional and the lender can pursue the guarantor before pursuing the company.

### Interest Rate
12.42% p.a. is relatively high for a new vehicle loan in 2025. Over the 60-month term with the balloon, total interest is $21,769.16 - approximately 42% of the principal loan amount.

### Balloon Payment
The $15,000 balloon at the end of term means the borrower will need to either:
1. Pay $15,020 lump sum
2. Refinance the balloon
3. Sell or trade in the vehicle

### Insurance Agreed Value vs Purchase Price
- Agreed insured value: $49,955.75
- Total purchase price: $56,500.01
- The insurance does NOT cover the full on-road purchase price. The gap (~$6,544) includes stamp duty, registration transfer, and some on-road costs.

### Missing Documents
The following documents you would normally expect are NOT in this pack:
1. **VicRoads Registration Certificate** - The official rego papers from Dept of Transport
2. **Contract of Sale** - Formal sale agreement between buyer and dealer
3. **Toyota New Vehicle Warranty** - 5-year/unlimited km warranty documentation
4. **Owner's Manual / Service Logbook** - Usually physical items
5. **Privacy Consent Forms** - Referenced in the application but not included in this pack
