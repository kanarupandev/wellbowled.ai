## Current Direction

The owner is exploring **transferring the VIC rego to NSW** (same corporate owner, employee driver in NSW). Key finding: **no stamp duty** on same-owner interstate transfer. Annual NSW rego will cost ~$400-600 more than VIC due to separate green slip + business motor vehicle tax surcharge. Full checklist in `09_transfer_vic_to_nsw_checklist.md`.
