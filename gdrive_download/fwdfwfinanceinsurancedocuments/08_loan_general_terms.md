### Part A - Conditions for Lending
- Lender only finances if: no default, all documents provided, confirmations are correct, and it's legal to do so

### Part B - What You Have to Pay

**Interest:**
- Fixed rate for the term (as stated in the Details)
- Accrues daily on the loan balance from settlement date
- Daily rate = annual rate / 365 (or 366 in leap year)
- Debited monthly to loan account
- Overdue rate = interest rate + 3% p.a.

**Extra Repayments:**
- Can make extra repayments at any time
- Extra repayments are **not refundable**
- May need to pay break costs if making extra repayments

**Payment Obligations:**
- Unconditional - cannot refuse payment even if vehicle is defective, damaged, lost, stolen, etc.
- Must pay in full, without set-off or deduction
- Plus GST if applicable

**Lender's Costs:**
- Borrower must pay any reasonable costs the lender incurs to enter into, administer, or enforce the agreement

### Part C - The Vehicle

**Ownership:**
- For a vehicle loan, the borrower owns the vehicle
- For a lease, the lender owns the vehicle

**Borrower Obligations:**
- Keep vehicle in good working order
- Protect from theft, loss, damage
- Notify lender of any defects, damage, injury, claims
- Pay all amounts related to the vehicle (taxes, registration, licence fees)
- Comply with all laws
- Keep vehicle registered
- Use predominantly for business operations
- Service and maintain per manufacturer recommendations
- Maintain and complete service records

**Insurance Requirements:**
- Must maintain insurance at all times
- Give lender details of each policy
- Provide certificate of currency if required
- Ensure policy covers lender's interest
- Notify lender of any claims
- Get lender's consent before settling claims
- Use claim proceeds for vehicle repair/replacement as directed by lender

**Restrictions (Cannot):**
- Create another security interest in the vehicle
- Dispose of or part with possession
- Lease or sublease to a third party (without consent)
- Move vehicle outside Australia (without consent)
- Use for rideshare/transport services
- Use in a way that could result in seizure
- Make changes likely to decrease value
- Change identifying plates or marks

**Inspections:**
- Lender can inspect vehicle up to 3 times per calendar year (unless default)
- During normal business hours with reasonable notice

### Part D - How the Loan Ends

**Normal End:** Last day of the term, after paying all amounts owing

**Early Termination:**
- Borrower can terminate early by paying the payout figure
- Lender can terminate early if there is a default
- On vehicle loss, the loan terminates

**Payout Figure:**
- Loan balance + accrued interest + early termination fee + break costs + all other amounts

### Part E - Default

**Events of Default:**
- Failure to pay on time
- Improper dealings with vehicle
- Insolvency of borrower or guarantor
- Creditor enforcement against borrower/guarantor
- Default under another arrangement with lender
- Illegality
- Unlawful behaviour
- Misrepresentation
- Unapproved use of vehicle loan
- Breach of vehicle obligations
- Loss of licences
- Change of ownership or control (without consent)

**Grace Period on Payment Default:**
- If paid within 2 business days of due date, lender will not notify of default or take enforcement action

**Lender's Remedies on Default:**
- Terminate agreement immediately
- Require payment of total amount owing
- Sue for unpaid amounts
- Enforce security interest
- Take enforcement proceedings
- Appoint a receiver
- Exercise power of attorney

### Part F - Guarantee

- Guarantor unconditionally guarantees payment of total amount owing
- Guarantor provides indemnity for any loss/costs from borrower's failure to pay
- Lender can claim against guarantor first (before pursuing borrower)
- Guarantor's liability not affected by any variations, releases, or concessions given to borrower
- Guarantor acts as principal debtor (must pay within 2 business days of demand)

### Part G - Other Terms

**Borrower Confirmations (given at signing, first day of term, and end of each month):**
- All information is complete and correct
- Not insolvent and no existing default
- Have obtained independent advice (or had opportunity to)
- Each person is responsible for total amount owing
- Have examined the vehicle and records
- Responsible for all licences/registrations

**Lender Can:**
- Act as principal or agent
- Transfer or assign rights
- Make changes to fees/charges with notice

**Borrower Cannot:**
- Transfer rights under the agreement without lender consent
