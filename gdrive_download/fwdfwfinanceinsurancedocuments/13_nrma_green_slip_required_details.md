## Potential Issue: Corporate Registration

Since this is a **company-owned vehicle** (AAS Security Group Pty Ltd), the green slip and NSW registration must be in the **company name**, not an individual's name. At Service NSW you may need:
- ABN certificate or ASIC company extract
- Proof that the person attending is authorised to act for the company (e.g. director — Barath Loganathan)
- If Kanarupan is attending (not Barath), a **letter of authority** from the director authorising Kanarupan to register the vehicle on behalf of AAS Security Group Pty Ltd
