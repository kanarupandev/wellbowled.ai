# Transferring RAV4 from VIC to NSW - What You Need

**Context:** The 2025 Toyota RAV4 Hybrid XSE (AWD), Rego DMQ226 (VIC), is currently registered in Victoria to AAS Security Group Pty Ltd. The company wants to re-register in NSW with new NSW plates and a green slip (CTP insurance). **Same corporate owner, no change of ownership** - employee driver will be based in NSW.

---

## What is a Green Slip?

In NSW, a **Green Slip** is Compulsory Third Party (CTP) insurance. Unlike Victoria where CTP (TAC) is bundled into your registration fee, in NSW you must purchase a green slip **separately** from a licensed CTP insurer before you can register or renew registration.

### Licensed NSW CTP Insurers
- Allianz
- GIO
- NRMA Insurance
- QBE
- Youi
- AAI (Suncorp)

You can compare green slip prices at [greenslips.nsw.gov.au](https://www.greenslips.nsw.gov.au)

## Steps to Re-register in NSW

### Step 1: Get a Green Slip (CTP Insurance)
- Purchase a NSW green slip from a licensed insurer **before** going to Service NSW
- You'll need: vehicle details (make, model, year, VIN), driver details, intended use
- The green slip insurer will issue a CTP certificate/number

### Step 2: Get a Blue Slip (Vehicle Inspection)
- Since this vehicle is coming from **interstate**, it requires a **Blue Slip** (not a Pink Slip)
- A Blue Slip is an identity and safety inspection for vehicles not currently registered in NSW
- Must be done at an **Authorised Unregistered Vehicle Inspection Station (AUVIS)**
- The inspection checks: identity (VIN, engine number, compliance plate), safety (brakes, lights, tyres, steering, etc.), and that the vehicle is not recorded as stolen or written off
- Cost: approximately ~$80
- Blue slip must be used within 42 days of issue

### Step 3: Cancel VIC Registration (or let it lapse)
- Cancel VIC registration (DMQ226) with VicRoads / Department of Transport Victoria
- You may be eligible for a **pro-rata refund** on the remaining VIC registration period
- Current VIC rego expires 26/03/2026 - so there is potentially ~12 months of refundable registration

### Step 4: Register at Service NSW
Visit a Service NSW centre with:

#### Documents Required

| Document | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Green Slip certificate** (CTP) | Need to purchase | Must be bought before registration |
| **Blue Slip** (inspection report) | Need to obtain | From an authorised inspection station, within 42 days |
| **Proof of identity** | Needed | 100 points of ID (driver's licence, passport, Medicare, etc.) |
| **Proof of NSW address** | Needed | Utility bill, bank statement, rental agreement |
| **VIC Registration Certificate** | **MISSING from pack** | The official VicRoads rego paper - critical for proving same-owner stamp duty exemption |
| **Retail Invoice / Receipt** | Have it | Retail Invoice RINTN02416 proves purchase details |
| **Evidence of VIN / Compliance Plate** | On the vehicle | Checked during Blue Slip inspection |
| **Financier's consent / letter** | Need to obtain | Since Angle Auto Finance has a security interest, they must consent to the state transfer and be noted on the NSW registration |
| **ABN certificate** | Needed | Registering under AAS Security Group in NSW |

### Step 5: Pay NSW Registration Fees
- Registration fee (~$82)
- Motor vehicle tax (weight-based, business rate)
- Number plate fee (~$55 for new NSW plates)
- Stamp duty: **$0** (same owner exemption - bring VIC rego certificate as proof)

## Important Considerations

### 1. Financier Notification (CRITICAL)
Angle Auto Finance holds a **first-ranking security interest** over this vehicle. The loan general terms (Part C) require you to:
- Keep the vehicle registered at all times
- Not change identifying plates without consent
- Notify the lender of any changes

**You MUST contact Angle Auto Finance before proceeding** to get their written consent for the interstate transfer and re-registration.

### 2. Insurance Update
Your current Toyota Insurance policy lists:
- Registration: DMQ226 (VIC)
- Vehicle usage: Private
- Address: 64 Maramba Dr, Narre Warren VIC 3805

Once re-registered in NSW, you must notify Toyota Insurance (137 200) to update:
- New NSW registration number
- New address
- Any change in vehicle usage

Note: A green slip is CTP only. You still need to maintain your **comprehensive insurance** (currently with Toyota Insurance) as a separate policy. The loan requires you to maintain comprehensive insurance at all times.

### 3. Stamp Duty
**$0** - Same corporate owner (AAS Security Group Pty Ltd) transferring interstate. VIC stamp duty ($2,276.40) was already paid. NSW does not charge stamp duty again when there is no change of ownership. Bring the VIC registration certificate and retail invoice as proof.

### 4. Timeline
- Cancel VIC rego and apply for refund
- Don't let the vehicle go unregistered - time the NSW registration to overlap or immediately follow VIC cancellation
- Blue Slip inspections can usually be booked within a few days
- Blue slip valid for 42 days from issue
