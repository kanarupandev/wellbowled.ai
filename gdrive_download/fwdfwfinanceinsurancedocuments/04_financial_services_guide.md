## Services Provided

TFAL is authorised to issue and provide general advice on:
- General insurance products
- Life insurance products (limited to consumer credit insurance)

The motor dealer (Nunawading Toyota) is an authorised representative of TFAL.

## Key Points

### Nature of Advice
- Advice provided is **general in nature** and does not take into account individual objectives, needs, or financial situation
- Customer should consider appropriateness of advice and obtain the PDS before purchasing

### Who TFAL Acts For
- TFAL has a binding authority with Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Company Australia Pty Ltd (Adica)
- When issuing insurance underwritten by Adica, TFAL acts for the insurer, not the customer
- TFAL also shares common ownership with Adica

### How TFAL is Paid
- Commission on new business and renewals, calculated as a percentage of the base premium (less GST and government charges), or a fixed dollar fee, or both
- TFAL may also receive profit share commissions from insurers based on portfolio performance

### Dealer Remuneration
- Motor dealer receives a flat fee, a commission (percentage of premium excl. taxes), or combination of both
- If salesperson is a Contractor, the dealer pays a proportion of their commission to the Contractor

### Salesperson Remuneration
- Salary paid by employer
- May receive performance bonuses
- May participate in rewards point programmes

### Soft Dollar Benefits
- Representatives receive soft dollar commissions from TFAL and insurers (conferences, travel, sponsorships, gifts)
