# Energy Flow v2 — Corrected Plan

## Core Principle
The kinetic chain order is FIXED biomechanics. Not detected from data.
Vision identifies WHEN (which frame). Velocity measures HOW MUCH (efficiency).
The ORDER is hardcoded knowledge.

## The 5 Phases (hardcoded)

1. GROUND CONTACT — back foot plants
2. FRONT FOOT BRACE — front foot plants, body decelerates
3. HIP ROTATION — hips open, shoulders still closed
4. TRUNK ROTATION — shoulders catch up to hips
5. ARM & RELEASE — arm whips over, wrist snaps

## Phase Detection (posture-based, not velocity-based)

Phase 1: back ankle (27/28) at lowest y, front ankle still elevated
Phase 2: front ankle (27) at lowest y, both feet grounded
Phase 3: maximum hip-shoulder angle separation (X-factor peak)
Phase 4: hip-shoulder separation collapsing (shoulders catching up)
Phase 5: bowling wrist (16) at highest point (arm over)

## Efficiency Measurement (velocity, only at identified frames)

At each phase frame, measure relevant segment velocity.
Transfer ratio = next_phase_velocity / current_phase_velocity.
