# Competitive Research — X-Factor Video Content

## Finding: The space is EMPTY

Searched for existing viral cricket bowling analysis videos with hip-shoulder separation angle overlays on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube. **None exist.**

### What exists today

| Source | Format | Audience | Accessible? |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| PitchVision | Coaching platform with angle analysis | Paid subscribers | No — paywall |
| Quintic Sports | Professional biomechanics software | Labs/academies | No — expensive software |
| CricFit blog | Text articles + static diagrams | Coaches | Free but no video |
| Academic papers | PDFs with motion capture data | Researchers | Journal access |
| Coaching YouTube | Manual slow-mo + voice-over | General | Free but no automated overlay |

### What does NOT exist (our opportunity)

- Automated hip-shoulder separation overlay on standard video (no motion capture needed)
- Real-time angle number on the bowler during slo-mo replay
- Comparison scale (amateur → elite) on a verdict card
- Social-media-ready 9:16 format with hook-analysis-verdict structure

### Closest competitors

1. **Cricket coaching YouTube channels** — manual analysis with arrows drawn in editing software. Slow to produce, inconsistent quality.
2. **Hawk-Eye / PitchVision** — professional grade but locked behind broadcast deals or subscriptions.
3. **General sports biomechanics accounts** — exist for golf, baseball (x-factor is well-known in golf). Nobody has adapted this for cricket at social media scale.

### The bar to beat

Since nobody does this for cricket, the bar is set by **golf x-factor content** and **baseball biomechanics** accounts on Instagram:
- Clean overlays, not cluttered
- One metric per video
- Comparison to pros is the share trigger
- 15-30s, punchy, no filler
- First frame must work as a thumbnail

### Our advantage

1. MediaPipe runs on any video — no special equipment needed
2. One Gemini Flash call identifies the bowler — works on any clip
3. Fully automated — minutes not hours per video
4. The 23-parameter DNA model gives us depth nobody else has
