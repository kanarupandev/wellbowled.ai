# Session Onboarding

## Purpose

This document is the fast handoff for a fresh agent or collaborator.
It summarizes the current business direction and points to the most important reference docs.

## Business Snapshot

The venture is currently best understood as:
- a bowling-specialized media + analysis brand
- website-first
- content-led
- initially solo-founder operated
- using proprietary internal tooling as leverage
- monetized through premium services rather than app-first SaaS

Current core channels:
- website
- YouTube
- Instagram
- Facebook

Current content context:
- IPL 2026 starts `2026-03-28`
- IPL season is a major attention window
- the business should use IPL as an attention engine, but not become too dependent on unowned broadcast footage

## Current Strategic Direction

### Brand
Current strongest umbrella-brand direction:
- `wellBowled.ai`
- `wellBowled.com` redirecting to `.ai`

Reason:
- cricket is already a niche
- bowling is already a narrow enough vertical
- the company needs room for multiple products and future expansion

### Product/Service Stack
Current core service logic:
1. DNA Signature
2. extracted-figure analysis techniques
3. detailed coach report

Interpretation:
- DNA Signature = acquisition layer
- extracted-figure analysis = differentiation layer
- coach report = monetization core

### Scope
Current recommended product scope:
- seam bowling first
- spin bowling later as a separate future expansion

## Business Setup Recommendation

Current best starter recommendation:
- use the existing sole trader ABN for now
- keep the venture operationally separate in records and bookkeeping
- validate demand first
- move to a Pty Ltd later when revenue/complexity justifies it

Important note:
- because the founder is on a 491 visa, migration/accounting advice should still be obtained once for confirmation

## Immediate Practical Recommendation

Priority order right now:
1. secure the domains
2. start under the current ABN
3. launch basic website/content presence
4. validate offers and demand
5. formalize company structure later if justified

## Current Core Rules

- narrow the business by vertical, not by brand
- do not build a generic cricket AI app
- do not depend too heavily on unowned IPL footage
- do not underprice serious services just to appear accessible
- do not wait for full automation to test the market
- use tooling as internal leverage, not as the whole business

## Short Summary

The current best business interpretation is:
- build a bowling-first brand
- use content as acquisition
- use the website as the owned conversion layer
- use premium services as monetization
- start narrow in scope but broad enough in brand to expand later
