# Market Analysis

## Timing

Current strategic window:
- IPL 2026 starts on `2026-03-28`
- IPL 2026 final is scheduled for `2026-05-31`

This creates a strong immediate attention window.

## Market Verdict

The market is attractive enough to build in.

The best opportunity is not a broad cricket app.
The best opportunity is a bowling-specialized media + service business.

## Main Positive Signals

- IPL attention is massive.
- Cricket dominates the Indian sports economy.
- Creator-led businesses are increasingly normal.
- Existing cricket-tech products prove demand exists.

## Main Existing Competition

Visible competitors include products such as:
- CricVision
- Pitchwolf
- HomeGround
- ActionLab Bowling Analyzer

This means the market already supports AI/analysis language.
It also means the business must not enter as a generic analysis app.

## Main Market Gap

The strongest identifiable gap is not generic analysis.
It is:
- bowling-specific identity + analysis + content

More specifically:
- a bowling-only identity product
- premium, visually strong bowling analysis outputs
- a bridge between consumer curiosity and coach-facing utility
- a website-led owned funnel around bowling as a vertical

## Main Risks

- dependence on unowned IPL broadcast footage
- weak differentiation if positioned as another generic cricket-tech app
- solo-founder overload if services are underpriced or too custom

## Best Initial Wedge

Best starting segment:
- serious amateur and junior fast bowlers
- ambitious bowlers
- coaches wanting premium visual reports

## Strategic Conclusion

Best business angle:
- bowling-first media and analysis brand
- content-led
- website-backed
- premium services on top
- proprietary tooling underneath
