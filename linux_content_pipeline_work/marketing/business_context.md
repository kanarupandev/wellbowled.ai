# Business Context

## Founder Context

Current founder profile:
- senior AI engineer
- extensive cricket knowledge
- initial solo-founder operating model

This is a meaningful advantage because the business requires both:
- real technical execution
- real cricket judgment

## Business Direction

The business is best understood as:
- a bowling-specialized media + analysis venture
- website-first
- content-led
- premium services layered on top

Primary channels:
- website
- YouTube
- Instagram
- Facebook

## Core Business Model

The business is not just one tool.
It is an umbrella venture that may eventually include:
- bowling DNA/signature products
- extracted-figure analysis products
- coach-facing reports
- content/media products
- clip management and future workflow tooling

## Strategic Principle

The technical pipeline is an internal advantage, not the business by itself.

Commercially, the business must sell outcomes such as:
- identity
- comparison
- analysis
- coaching value
- premium content

Not just:
- correctness of internal analysis architecture

## Main Risk

The biggest risk is not technical failure.
It is building a technically impressive system before proving:
- who pays
- what they pay for
- what is repeatable as a business

## Best Commercial Structure

The strongest structure is:
- content as acquisition
- website as owned funnel
- services as monetization
- tooling as hidden leverage

## Operating Rule

**Do not wait for full automation to test the market.**
