# Bumrah vs Steyn — What

## Purpose

Define what the first flagship video actually is before moving into scripting or technical execution.

## The Video

This video is:

- a short visual comparison
- between Bumrah and Steyn
- built around a strong contradiction
- designed to make one idea obvious very quickly

## Core Content Promise

`I will show you why Bumrah feels strange, and why that does not stop him from being elite.`

## Core Idea

Two elite fast bowlers.
Very different actions.
Same bowling problem solved in different ways.

## Core Takeaway

Different is not broken.
Elite bowling does not have one single textbook look.

## What The Viewer Should See

1. Bumrah looks visually unusual.
2. Steyn looks visually classical.
3. The contrast is obvious when shown side by side.
4. Unusual does not mean ineffective.

## What The Video Must Do

1. Hook curiosity immediately.
2. Show Bumrah's unusual look clearly.
3. Show Steyn as the contrast point.
4. Make the difference legible in matched visual moments.
5. Reframe the difference as elite variation, not error.

## What The Video Must Not Do

- become a legality debate
- become a biomechanics lecture
- prescribe a correction
- tell the viewer to copy Bumrah
- rely on too many numbers
- overclaim scientific certainty

## Content Scope

The first version should stay focused on:

- visible difference
- visual contrast
- one strong reframe

It should not try to explain everything about either bowler.

## Best Emotional Arc

Beginning:
`This looks wrong.`

Middle:
`Now put him next to a textbook bowler.`

End:
`Different does not mean broken.`
