## Premium Visual Treatment

The overall look must feel high-end sports analysis, not amateur cricket YouTube.

### Colour palette

- **Background tone:** Dark. Use deep charcoal or near-black behind frames and text. Never bright or busy.
- **Text colour:** Clean white or off-white. No coloured text. No drop shadows unless needed for legibility over footage.
- **Skeleton overlay colours:** Muted, not neon. Amber, cyan, green as listed above — but at ~70% saturation, not full blast. The overlay should feel like a professional broadcast tool, not a TikTok filter.

### Typography

- One typeface only. Sans-serif, medium weight. No italics, no bold variation mid-sentence.
- Text appears with a clean fade or cut-in. No bounce, no typewriter effect, no kinetic text.
- Generous line spacing. Never crowd lines together.
- Text positioned in the lower third or centred — never floating at random positions.

### Frame treatment

- All footage frames should have a consistent crop and slight grade: desaturate slightly, lift blacks, add subtle vignette. This unifies the two clips visually even though they come from different sources/quality levels.
- Side-by-side card: thin vertical divider line (1-2px, white, 50% opacity). Both bowlers scaled to similar height in frame. Equal padding.

### Transitions

- Dissolve between Bumrah frames in Beat 2. Clean and slow.
- Hard cut to Steyn in Beat 3. The contrast in energy is intentional.
- Fade to dark for close. No wipe, no slide, no zoom transition.

### Motion

- Slow-mo frames: subtle Ken Burns drift (very slight zoom or pan over 2 seconds). Keeps the frame alive without feeling animated.
- Side-by-side card: static. No movement. Let the comparison do the work.
- Real-time replay in Beat 4: full speed, no effects. Raw contrast after the slow breakdown.

### Sound (if added)

- Deep, minimal beat. Sub-bass presence. No melody.
- Beat drops or swells aligned to: hook reveal (0:00), Steyn hard cut (0:10), real-time replay (0:22).
- Silence or near-silence on the close card. Let the text land.

### What to avoid

- No lens flares, light leaks, or particle effects
- No coloured backgrounds or gradients behind text
- No emoji, icons, or graphic elements
- No subscribe/like overlays
- No watermark other than wellBowled.ai on the close card
- Nothing that signals "template" or "Canva"
