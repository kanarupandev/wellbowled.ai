# Bumrah vs Steyn — Duration

## Goal

Choose the strongest duration for the first version of:

`Bumrah vs Steyn: Why Bumrah Feels Wrong... But Still Works`

## Recommended First Release Length

`35-40 seconds`

## Why This Is The Best Range

This topic has:

- one strong contradiction
- one strong visual comparison
- one clear takeaway

That makes it ideal for a tight short-form edit.

## Why Not Longer First

If the first version gets too long, it risks becoming:

- less punchy
- less replayable
- less shareable
- more educational than viral

## Length By Goal

### Most viral

`30-45 seconds`

Best for:

- YouTube Shorts
- Instagram Reels
- quick retention
- one-idea clarity

### Viral plus slightly more authority

`45-60 seconds`

Best if:

- the visual material is unusually strong
- the explanation still stays very tight

### Expanded authority version

`75-120 seconds`

Better as:

- a second version
- a YouTube-specific expanded cut
- a later follow-up

Not the best first breakout cut.

## Best Practical Recommendation

First publish:

`35-40 seconds`

That is long enough to:

- hook
- compare
- reveal
- close

And short enough to:

- keep retention high
- feel sharp
- encourage replay

## Suggested Internal Shape

- `0-3s` hook
- `3-10s` show Bumrah unusual
- `10-20s` compare to Steyn
- `20-32s` explain why different still works
- `32-40s` close with the core reframe
