# wellBowled.ai — Top 5 Monetizable Content Gaps

## Core View

The opportunity is not generic cricket content.

The opportunity is to own the category where:

- bowling research
- visual explanation
- personal analysis
- and useful product features

all connect into one brand.

## Top 5 Gaps

### 1. Visual bowling intelligence for normal cricketers

This is the biggest gap.

Most cricket analysis content focuses on:

- international stars
- opinions
- generic coaching advice

Very little is built for:

- club bowlers
- junior bowlers
- parents
- recreational players
- coaches who want visual proof

This is commercially strong because it serves people who can actually become users and customers.

### 2. Research-to-visual cricket content

Academic papers and biomechanics research exist, but they are not being translated into:

- short-form visual explanations
- simple repeatable content formats
- usable insights for real bowlers

This is a strong brand gap because it gives:

- authority
- uniqueness
- intellectual credibility
- reusable content ideas

### 3. Personalized bowling analysis

General cricket content gets attention.
Personalized content gets revenue.

The real value is:

- your action
- your speed
- your bowling DNA
- your comparison to elite bowlers
- your change over time

This is the strongest monetization bridge from content to product.

### 4. Progress tracking, not one-off analysis

Most sports content is one-time consumption.

What is missing is:

- stored sessions
- simple performance history
- progress trends
- repeated use around improvement

This matters because retention is built through tracking, not through one-off novelty.

### 5. Premium biomechanics media in cricket

There is a lot of cricket media.
There is very little premium, visually distinctive, intellectually serious bowling biomechanics media.

That is an opportunity because premium media can do two jobs:

- build a trusted brand
- feed users into paid analysis and product layers

## Ranked By Long-Term Commercial Value

1. Personalized bowling analysis
2. Progress tracking
3. Visual bowling intelligence for normal cricketers
4. Research-to-visual cricket content
5. Premium biomechanics media

## Bottom Line

The biggest opportunity is:

build a trusted visual bowling-intelligence brand, then turn that trust into personalized analysis, progress tracking, and premium AI layers.
